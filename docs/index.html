Learning OS

A stateful tutoring skill for children and adults: diagnosis, mastery maps, spaced retrieval, misconception repair, and coach notes.

What It Is

Learning OS turns a folder into a long-term learning workspace. Instead of asking an AI to explain a topic once, it gives the tutor durable state: mission, learner profile, diagnostic, mastery map, review schedule, misconceptions, practice bank, lessons, and coach notes.

diagnose map teach one thing practice retrieve later update model

What It Can Do

DiagnoseFind the learner's knowledge frontier before teaching.
MapRepresent prerequisites, component skills, evidence, and mastery levels.
RetainSchedule retrieval practice so learning survives forgetting.
RepairTrack recurring misconceptions and design contrast examples.
AdaptUse Adult, Child, or Mixed Mode depending on the learner.
CoachSeparate learner-facing instruction from parent, teacher, or manager notes.

Use It For

  • Adult technical learning: TypeScript, SQL, AI agents, finance, writing, research.
  • Children's learning: fractions, reading comprehension, English, science, homework support.
  • Parent-assisted learning: short lessons plus actionable parent notes.
  • Exam prep: syllabus mapping, cumulative review, weak-topic diagnosis.
  • Professional training: team enablement, internal curriculum, sales and writing practice.

Expected Effects

Learning OS is designed to improve retention, transfer, confidence, progression, and coaching quality. It does this by diagnosing before teaching, teaching one thing at a time, requiring evidence before raising mastery, and scheduling retrieval after the learner starts forgetting.

这是什么

Learning OS 是一个把文件夹变成长期学习工作区的技能。它不是让 AI 一次性解释知识点，而是把学习目标、学习者画像、诊断、掌握地图、复习计划、误区、练习题库、课程和家长/教师笔记都保存下来。

诊断 建图 只教一件事 练习 延迟提取 更新模型

它能做什么

诊断在正式教学前找到学习者的知识边界。
掌握地图记录前置知识、组成技能、证据和掌握程度。
长期记忆安排提取练习，而不是只让学习者重读。
修复误区把重复错误当作信号，追踪并修正核心误解。
适配支持成人模式、儿童模式和家长/老师协作模式。
教练笔记把面向学习者的内容和面向家长/老师的建议分开。

适用场景

  • 成人技术学习：TypeScript、SQL、AI Agent、金融、写作、研究。
  • 儿童学习：分数、阅读理解、英语、科学、作业辅导。
  • 家长陪伴：短课程加上可执行的家长建议。
  • 考试准备：知识点地图、累计复习、弱点诊断。
  • 企业培训：团队赋能、销售训练、写作训练。
